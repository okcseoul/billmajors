About This Book

Every person carries a story of what a father was — or wasn't. The Half Apple is the story of a boy whose father walked away, and how he discovered that God was not absent, not distant, but present as a Father in ways no human father could be.

Written by Bill R. Majors — a missionary who has served in Seoul, Korea since 1982 — this short book has traveled quietly from person to person, finding its way into the hands of those who needed it most.
