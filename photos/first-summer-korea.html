August 1982 Korea

First Summer in Korea

Bill arrived in Korea on June 2, 1982. By August, he was already teaching.

This is the Korean teachers' seminar — middle and high school teachers gathered for an English as a Second Language program, and Bill standing before them just weeks after landing. He had come to learn a country, and the country was already teaching him back.

The stamp in the corner reads '82.8 — August 1982. He had been in Korea less than three months.
